🤖 How It Works

  1. Upload a classroom photo
  2. AI detects all faces using HOG detector
  3. Each face is encoded into 128 dimensions
  4. Compared against enrolled student embeddings
  5. Matched students → Present
  6. Unmatched students → Absent
  7. You can manually correct any result
Tip: For best results, ensure good lighting and that student faces are clearly visible in the photo.
